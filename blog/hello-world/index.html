Welcome to the official Carolina Cloud blog! We’re excited to share our journey building a family-owned cloud platform that puts transparency, performance, and affordability first.

Why We Built Carolina Cloud

As researchers, we experienced firsthand the frustrations of traditional cloud providers: confusing pricing, long cluster queues, and bills that spiraled out of control. We knew there had to be a better way.

Carolina Cloud was born from a simple idea: what if cloud computing was transparent, affordable, and actually designed for the people who use it?
