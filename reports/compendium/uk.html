    An absolute titan of a bank, HSBC is a British multinational investment bank and financial services holding company. It is the second largest bank in Europe with total assets of US$2.984 trillion as of December 2020. It is a truly global bank with truly global reach, therefore it has experience with digital transformations in a wide variety of countries, serving a wide variety of customers.

    HSBC has around 3,900 offices in 65 countries and territories across Africa, Asia, Oceania, Europe, North America, and South America, and around 38 million customers. As of 2014, it was the world's sixth-largest public company, according to a composite measure by Forbes magazine. It is considered a systemically important bank by the Financial Stability Board.

    The bank is organized in four business groups: commercial banking, global banking and markets (investment banking), retail banking and wealth management.

    Operating Income by department

    The Wealth and Personal Banking divison reported the largest operating income in 2020 of $21.999B, followed by Global Banking and Markets with $14.994B, Commercial Banking with $13.294B and Corporate Center with $142M.

    Operating Income by region

    HSBC reports its largest operating income in Asia by $26.922B. In Europe it generates a total operating income of $18.419B, in North America $6.375B, Latin America $3.020B, and MENA $2.628B.

    Net Income before taxes by department

    Net Income before taxes in 2020 was strongest in the International Retail Banking and Financial Services department by $3.704B followed by Global Banking and Markets at $3.616B and Commercial Banking at $1.639B and negative for Corporate Center at $-182M.

    Standard Chatered is a British multinational bank and financial services company headquartered in London. Its branch network encompasses 1,200 branches and outlets across more than 70 countries, employing around 87,000 people. It is a universal bank and offers consumer, corporate and institutional banking, and treasury services. Despite being based in the UK, Standard Chartered does not conduct retail banking in the UK and around 90% of its profits come from Asia, Africa and the Middle East. This makes it quite an interesting case study, especially with respect to its digital strategy. Though not nearly as hefty as HSBC it is nonetheless considered a systematically imporatant bank by the Financial Stability Board.

    Operating Income by department

    The Corporate, Commercial & Institutional Banking divison reported the largest operating income in 2020 of $8.485B, followed by Consumer, Private and Business Banking with $5.691B, and Central & other items with $589B.

    Operating Income by region

    Standard Chartered reports its largest operating income in Asia by $10.382B. In Africa and the Middle East it generates a total operating income of $2.364B. In Europe & America $1.922B and in Central & other items it reports $97M.

    Net Income before taxes by department

    Net Income before taxes in 2020 was strongest in the Corporate, Commercial & Institutional Banking divison by $1.773B followed by Consumer, Private and Business Banking at $649M and was negative for Central & other items at $-809M.

    Barclays is a British multinational bank that provides numerous financial products and services in the U.K., rest of Europe, the Americas, Africa, the Middle East, and Asia. The bank operates as two divisions: Barclays UK and Barclays International. It offers financial services, such as retail banking, credit cards, wholesale banking, investment banking, wealth management, and investment management services. The company also engages in securities dealing activities; and issues credit cards. The company was formerly known as Barclays Bank Limited and changed its name to Barclays PLC in January 1985. Barclays PLC was founded in 1690 and is headquartered in London, the United Kingdom.

    Barclays has a primary listing on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 100 Index. It has a secondary listing on the New York Stock Exchange.

    Operating Income by department

    Barclays International divison reported the largest operating income in 2020 of $20.442B, followed by Barclays UK with $8.149B and its Head office with $-644M.

    Operating Income by region

    Barclays reports its largest operating income in the UK with $14.394B. In the Amercias and the Rest of Europe it generates a total operating income of $9.553B and $2.643B, respectively. In Asia, it amounts to $1.328B and in Africa and the Middle East $46M.

    Net Income before taxes by department

    Net Income before taxes in 2020 was strongest in the Barclays International with $4.642B. Barclays UK generated $701M of operating income in 2020 and it was negative for Head office at $-1.408B.

    Lloyds Bank is a British financial institution formed through the acquisition of HBOS by Lloyds TSB in 2009. It's heritage dates back over 320 years and is now headquartered in London. Lloyds Banking Group is one of the UK's largest financial service companies with 30M customers and 65,000 employees.

    The bank operates through two segments, Retail and Commercial Banking. The Retail segment offers various financial service products, including current accounts, savings, mortgages, car finance, and unsecured consumer lending to personal and small business customers. The Commercial Banking segment provides lending, transactional banking, working capital management, risk management, and debt capital markets services to startups, SMEs, corporates, and financial institutions.

    Operating Income by department

    The Retail Banking divison reported the largest operating income in 2020 of $12.911B, followed by Commercial Banking with $3.817B and Other with $1.997B.

    Net Income before taxes by department

    Net Income before taxes in 2020 was strongest for the Retail Banking department with $2.382B followed by Commercial Banking at $25M and was negative for Other at $702M.
