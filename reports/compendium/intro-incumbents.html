Intro

    Incumbent banks across the globe are facing significant challenges since the past few years. The disruption of the industry by the rapidly rising number of challenger banks offering more customer-centric and user-friendly digital products and services has led incumbents to reconsider their business model and offerings.

    Customers are less willing to visit branches to manage their finances. Instead, they like the convenience of using their bank's app or platform on their mobile devices or computers. Certainly, Covid-19 reinforced this trend and incumbents were faced the necessity to invest substantial amounts into their tech stacks to further improve their back-end as well as front-end client interaction.

Deutsche Bank is an international bank headquartered in Frankfurt. It's customers range from private, wealthy, corporates and institutional clients as well as the public sector.

In the private client section, the bank offers accounts and cards, save and investment plans, credit and real estates next to insurance and provision to its clients.

For its wealthy clients, i.e. family offices, foundations, churches, private persons it offers asset and wealth management, risk management, advice on financing and real estate among others.

Société Générale, often called SocGen is a French multinational investment bank and financial services company headquartered in Paris. The company is a universal bank and counts the divisions French Networks, Global Transaction Banking, International Retail Banking, Financial Services, Corporate and Investment Banking, Private Banking, Asset Management and Securities Services among their services.

SocGen is France's third largest bank by total assets after BNP Paribas and Crédit Agricole. It also the sixth largest bank in Europe and the world's eighteenth. The company is a component of the Euro Stoxx 50 stock market index. It is considered a systemically important bank by the Financial Stability Board. It is known as one of the Trois Vieilles ("Old Three") of French banking, along with BNP Paribas and Crédit Lyonnais.
